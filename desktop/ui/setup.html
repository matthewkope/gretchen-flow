👋 Welcome to Gretchen Flow

Press a hotkey, speak, and your words appear wherever your cursor is.

  1. Download a speech model. Gretchen ships without one so you can choose. The recommended model is Large v3 Turbo (quantized) — about 547 MB, the best balance of accuracy and speed. Click the button below, or pick any model from the menu bar icon’s Model submenu. The Gretchen icon shows a ↓ while it downloads.
  2. Grant macOS permissions. Gretchen needs three, all under System Settings ▸ Privacy & Security. Click each button to open the right pane and toggle Gretchen Flow on:
    Microphone = hear you · Accessibility = type the text · Input Monitoring = detect the Fn key. Relaunch Gretchen after enabling Input Monitoring.
  3. Dictate. Hold your hotkey (default the 🌐 Globe/Fn key), speak, then release — your words type out where the cursor is. Change or add hotkeys anytime from the window’s Shortcuts section.
    Tip: for the 🌐 Globe/Fn key, also set System Settings ▸ Keyboard ▸ Press 🌐 to: Do Nothing.
